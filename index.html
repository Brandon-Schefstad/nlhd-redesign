Policies

  1. A $20 non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking. Deposits made will go towards service. Send deposit via CashApp to $MissKeniaT
  2. A $15 late fee will be applied after 15 minutes. After another 15 minutes the appointment will be cancelled.
  3. If you cannot make an appointment, you must cancel 24 hours prior to the start time. You can reschedule with a transferred deposit within a 7 day period.
  4. Please come with your hair washed, pressed out, and with no chemicals.
  5. Please come picture ready.
  6. No children at the appointment unless service is being rendered.
